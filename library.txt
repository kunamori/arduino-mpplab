Keypad by Mark Stanley, Alexander Brevig

MFRC522 by GithubCommunity
# Version: 1.4.10 or later
# Used in:  lab-w5-2, lab-w5-3

Servo by Michael Margolis, Arduino
# Version: Built-in (no installation needed)

WiFi by Arduino
# Version: Built-in for ESP32 (no installation needed)
